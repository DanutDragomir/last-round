Privacy Policy

Last Round Card Game  ·  Last updated: June 2026

Last Round is a card game developed by a solo indie developer based in Romania. This policy explains what data is collected, why, and how it is used.

1. Data We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to run the game:

  • Account data — if you sign in with Google or create an account, we store your display name, email address, and a unique user ID.
  • Game data — scores, league progress, challenge results, and game preferences stored to your account.
  • Usage data — anonymous analytics (session length, feature usage) collected via Firebase Analytics to improve the game.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect payment information.
  • We do not sell or share your data with advertisers.
  • We do not collect location data.
  • We do not collect contacts or device identifiers beyond what Firebase requires.

3. Third-Party Services

Last Round uses the following services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Firebase (Google) — authentication, real-time database, analytics. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Google Sign-In — optional sign-in method. Google Privacy Policy

4. Guest Play

You can play Last Round as a guest without creating an account. In guest mode, no personal data is collected or stored beyond the current session.

5. Data Retention

Your account data is retained for as long as your account exists. You may request deletion at any time by emailing us.

6. Children

Last Round is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

7. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Union or Romania, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Object to processing of your data
  • Request data portability

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email below.

8. Security

We use Firebase's built-in security rules and Google Cloud infrastructure to protect your data. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect your information.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the game evolves. Significant changes will be communicated in-game or via email if you have an account. The date at the top of this page always reflects the latest revision.
