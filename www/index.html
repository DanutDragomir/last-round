📜 Last Round — Terms & Conditions

Last updated: June 2026

1. Account Inactivity

Accounts that have not been used for more than 30 days will be automatically signed out. Your progress and stats are preserved in the cloud — simply sign back in to continue playing.

Accounts inactive for more than 6 months will have their profile, stats, and leaderboard entries permanently deleted. This action cannot be undone. We will not retain any personal data after deletion.

2. Guest Play

Playing as a Guest does not require an account. Guest sessions are temporary — your wins, stats, and identity are not saved and will be wiped when you navigate away from or close the game.

3. Account Security

You are responsible for the security of your account credentials. Last Round accounts created with email and password should use a strong, unique password. We recommend Google Sign-In for added security. We will never ask for your password via chat or email.

4. Username & Avatar

Your chosen username and avatar are permanent and cannot be changed after registration. These form your identity on the Last Round leaderboard and in duels. Choose carefully.

5. Fair Play

Cheating, exploiting bugs, or using automation to manipulate leaderboard standings is prohibited and may result in immediate account removal without notice.

6. Data & Privacy

Last Round stores your username, avatar, game statistics, and authentication data using Firebase (Google Cloud). If you register with email, that email is stored securely by Firebase Auth. Google Sign-In uses Google's own authentication infrastructure. We do not share your data with third parties.

7. Changes to These Terms

These terms may be updated as the game evolves. Continued use of Last Round after updates are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised terms. Significant changes will be communicated in-game.

Last Round is an independent game project. For questions, reach out through the game community.

📖 Last Round — Rules

Empty all your cards — hand, face-up, then blind. The LAST player still holding cards is the loser!

🔑 4The key — whoever holds it must open the game. No 4? The next lowest card opens instead.
🍺 2Fresh Pint — the next player can play absolutely anything.
🎯 3Bull's Eye — draw a replacement card. You keep your turn and can play again!
🪞 5Bar Mirror — copies the card below it. Does not count toward four-of-a-kind.
🤫 6Sneaky — slides under the 7 restriction when needed.
🔔 7Last Orders — next player must play a 4 or a 6 (going down).
🥔 8 / 9Burnt potato — the most worthless cards in the deck. Play them to get rid of them!
💥 10Keg Blast — blows up the play pile. You get another turn.
🤡👑💅🌟 J Q K ACourt cards — must play equal or higher than the top card.
🔥 4-of-a-kindFour identical cards on top → pile burns! You play again. Four 🥔🥔🥔🥔 triggers MEGA BURN!
⬆ Pick UpCan't play? Pick up the whole pile. Use ⇅ Sort to tidy your new hand.
🂠 BlindLast resort — flip blind cards one at a time. A 🎁 gives another flip; anything unplayable makes you pick up!
