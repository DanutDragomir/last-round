Privacy Policy

The short version: We collect the minimum data needed to run the game. We do not sell your information. Your data is stored on EU servers. You can ask us to delete everything at any time.

What Data We Collect and Why

If You Sign In with Google

When you choose to sign in with your Google account, we receive and store:

DataWhy We Need It
Display nameShown on your player profile, leaderboard, and in multiplayer rooms
Google UIDA unique identifier that links your account across sessions — we never store your email or password
Avatar selectionYour chosen in-game avatar, stored so it appears consistently
Win/loss recordYour game statistics for the leaderboard and personal profile

We do not collect your email address, phone number, payment information, location, contacts, or any other personal details from your Google account.

If You Play as a Guest

If you play without signing in, no account is created on our servers. Your display name and game state are stored only on your device using localStorage — they never leave your phone unless you sign in later.

Game Activity Data

Across all players, we track win/loss counts, leaderboard rankings, challenge records, and season participation. This data is tied to your display name.

Technical Data (Firebase)

Firebase may collect standard technical information such as crash reports and performance diagnostics to help us fix bugs. This is processed by Google as a service provider on our behalf.

How Your Data Is Stored

All authenticated player data is stored in Firebase Realtime Database, configured to use the europe-west1 (Belgium) region — your data physically resides within the European Union.

Data is transmitted over TLS (HTTPS) and encrypted at rest. We do not run our own servers or maintain separate databases outside of Firebase.

How Long We Keep Your Data

Data TypeRetention
Authenticated player profileKept while your account is active
Guest profileOn your device only; deleted when you clear app data
Firebase diagnostic logsPer Google's standard policies (typically 90 days)

If you request deletion, we remove your profile data from Firebase within 30 days.

Who We Share Data With

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to any third party.

The only external service that processes your data is Google / Firebase, acting as our infrastructure provider. We do not use any advertising networks, analytics platforms beyond Firebase diagnostics, or marketing tools.

Google Sign-In

Authentication is handled by Google's OAuth 2.0 infrastructure. We receive only a UID and display name. We never see your Google password. You can revoke access at any time via your Google Account security settings.

Local Storage (Guest Mode)

Guest data is stored in your device's localStorage. This data never leaves your device, is not accessible to us, and can be cleared by clearing the app's data in your device settings.

Your Rights Under GDPR

If you are in the EU/EEA, you have the right to:

  • Access — ask what data we hold about you
  • Rectification — ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure — ask us to delete your account and all data
  • Portability — receive a copy of your data
  • Restriction — limit how we process your data
  • Object — object to processing based on legitimate interests

To exercise any right, email dragomirdanutmarian@googlemail.com. We respond within 30 days.

You may also lodge a complaint with Romania's supervisory authority: ANSPDCP — dataprotection.ro

How to Request Account Deletion

Email dragomirdanutmarian@googlemail.com with subject "Account Deletion Request" and your in-game display name. We will delete your data and confirm within 30 days.

Children's Privacy

Last Round is rated for users 13 years of age and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe your child has created an account, contact us immediately and we will delete it.

Changes to This Policy

Material changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the game after changes are posted constitutes acceptance.
